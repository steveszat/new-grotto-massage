Understanding Pressure Points

In Chinese massage, pressure points are specific spots on the body believed to be connected to the flow of energy, or qi. Stimulating these points can help restore balance, improve circulation, and promote healing.

What Are Pressure Points?

Pressure points, also known as acupressure points, relate to the body’s meridians or energy pathways. Targeting these points during a massage can release blocked energy and alleviate various conditions.
