About Chinese Massage

Chinese massage is more than relaxation — it's a form of traditional medicine practiced for over 2,000 years. Unlike Western massage, which focuses mainly on muscles, Chinese techniques work to restore the body’s energy balance and stimulate natural healing.

At New Grotto Massage, we use authentic methods like Tui Na, a hands-on technique that targets pressure points, relieves tension, and improves circulation. Many of our clients find that regular Chinese massage helps reduce chronic pain, boost energy, and calm the mind.

Come experience the deep healing and balance that only traditional Chinese massage can offer.
