Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome.

Can I call ahead to check availability?

Yes, feel free to call to ensure someone is available.

How long is each session?

Most massages are 30, 60, 90, or 120 minutes. Combo massage is 60 minutes (30 foot + 30 body).

What types of massage do you offer?

We offer Traditional Chinese Massage, Deep Tissue Massage, Thai Massage, and Combo Massage.

How much does a massage cost?

Prices range from $50 to $150 depending on the type and duration. Four-hand massage is available at double the regular price.

Are your therapists licensed?

Yes, all of our massage therapists are licensed and trained.

Is your facility clean and sanitary?

Absolutely. Cleanliness is a top priority here. Every room is thoroughly cleaned between sessions, and fresh linens are always used.

Do you offer anything extra or “full service”?

No. We are a professional massage business. Any inappropriate requests will result in refusal of service.
